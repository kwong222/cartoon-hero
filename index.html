Spider-Man

About Spider-Man

Spider-Man is one of the best superheroes in Marvel Comics, who has fought numerous bad guys. He is a very relatable character and has spider-powers, like climbing up walls, super-strength, and he invented web-shooters that shoot artificial webs. Spider-man's secret identity is Peter Parker. He let a thief go past him when he was trying to make some money, because he couldn't be bothered to catch the thief. However, the thief ended up killing his uncle Ben. He fights crime for that reason, and his motto is: with great power, there must also come great responsibility, a quote that is often written wrongly.

Personal life and personality of Peter Parker (Spider-Man)

He was married to Mary-Jane Watson, who he met in high-school. hoever, in exchange for saving his Aunt May's life, he sold his marriage to the devil, Mephisto. He is a science whiz and started up a company, Parker Industries, but had to sacrifice it to stop a bad guy, Doctor Octopus. His girlfriend in high-school, Gwen Stacy, was famously killed at the hands of the Green Goblin. He had many jobs, starting as a photographer for the Daily Bugle, where he took photos of his alter-ego, Spider-Man, for money. He was also a teacher at the high school he studied in.

Friends of Spider-Man

Peter has many awesome friends from university and his job at the Bugle. His best friend is Harry Osborn, but his father's secret identity was the Green Goblin and after a bout of drug problems, Harry also became the Goblin. His other friend who was always there for him was Flash Thompson, but he was killed by Norman Osborn as the Red Goblin. He also has many friends that he made during his web-slinging persona, notably the Fantastic Four and the black cat, who he dated for a while (the Black Cat, not the Fantastic Four).
